The Mission

AI on Asset is the first ever artificial intelligence defect detection platform using images captured from cameras based on UAV,Helicopters and ground for the utilities. Aim Asset is on a mission to automate defect detection on asset through images primarily catering to Power,Oil and Gas and Infrastructure sector. It serves a highly intelligent and sophisticated tool for infra companies ,which can scan through huge amount of image data and converting them into meaningful insights for maintenance.These eliminates the data inaccuracy and time consuming tasks that currently limit the operation and maintenance of Assets. It helps infra sector to increase asset utilization at minimum cost,thus putting them on an accelerated path of revenue.
